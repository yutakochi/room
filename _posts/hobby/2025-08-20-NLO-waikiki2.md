Here, I will introduce some photos unrelated to the conference.

Lately, with the yen being so weak, I expected fewer Japanese tourists in Hawaii. Surprisingly, though, there were still a lot of Japanese families with kids around. In fact, the proportion of Japanese visitors was higher than any other overseas destination I’ve been to.
Going to Hawaii for summer vacation with your family—now that’s really living it up! The weather was much cooler and more comfortable compared to Japan, so it almost felt like a summer resort to escape the heat.
