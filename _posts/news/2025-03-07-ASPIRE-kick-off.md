I attended the kick-off meeting of the JST ASPIRE Project, a collaboration between Keio University and the Technical University of Denmark (DTU). (Mar 4-7, 2025)

From Keio University, a total of 10 members from the Takeoka and Hayase Labs participated, while 19 members from the DTU Quantum Physics and Information Technology Group (U. L. Andersen), including four Associate Professors, joined from Denmark. I gave a 20-minute presentation introducing my research.

For more photos about this trip, [click here]({{ site.baseurl }}/travel/Aspire-kick-off2/).
