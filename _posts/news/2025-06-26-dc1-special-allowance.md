## English Translation

**Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS)  
Division for Researcher Development, Fellowship Program Section**

**Notice on the Provision of Special Research Incentive for Fellows – DC in the Final Year of Appointment**

We sincerely appreciate your continued cooperation in the smooth operation of this program.

The Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS) provides a Special Research Incentive to Fellows – DC who are in the final year of their appointment, in order to support them in concentrating fully on producing excellent research outcomes, such as their doctoral dissertation. This initiative, starting in fiscal year 2024, aims to encourage Fellows – DC to engage even more proactively in research and achieve significant results.

Eligible recipients are those Fellows – DC in their final year of appointment (excluding those who have changed qualifications to PD) who have demonstrated outstanding research achievements during their fellowship period and are expected to make further progress.
