I gave a 25-minute invited talk at the **JSAP–Optica Joint Symposia** in Niigata, Japan (Sep 16–20, 2024).  

**Presentation title:**  
> *Demonstration of Photon-echo Based Quantum Memory and Up-conversion Single-photon Detector for Femtosecond Regime Communications*

It was my first invited talk, and since it was part of a joint symposium with *Optica*, the presentation was given in English.

Niigata once served as Japan’s main port connecting the country to the Eurasian continent before World War II, and was one of the most populous cities in Japan at that time.  
Because of this, the city retains a rich blend of cultures and many historical buildings.
