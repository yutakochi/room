I gave a 25-minute invited talk at The 13th Asia-Pacific Laser Symposium (APLS) 2025 in Jeju, Korea (May 13-17, 2025).  

**Presentation title:**
> *Femtosecond-Regime Photon Echo Generation from Quantum Dots and Detection Using Frequency Up-Conversion Technique*

I had previously given an invited talk in English in 2024, but this was my first invited talk abroad.
All oral presentations at this conference are invited talks, and general participants present only posters.

Jeju Island (済州島) is considered a tropical resort in Korea and is the only place in the country where mandarins can be grown, as most of the mainland experiences harsh winters. There were many groups of what appeared to be school trip students at the airport, giving the island a similar vibe to Okinawa in Japan. Interestingly, the latitude is similar to Tokyo, and the weather was actually cooler than Tokyo's.
