I presented my research orally at the JSAP Autumn Meeting in Noda Campus, Tokyo University of Science, Japan (Mar 14-17, 2025).  

**Presentation title:**
> *Beam diameter optimization for femtosecond sum-frequency generation in single-photon detection*

**Poster title:**
> *Optimizing sample of InAs quantum dots with resonator for improving photon echo generation efficiency*

This time, I participated in both the oral and poster sessions. It was a very busy March, as I had also been on a business trip to Denmark until just a week before.
